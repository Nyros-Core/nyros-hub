Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This privacy policy applies to the artificial intelligence Nyros ("Nyros AI"), developed as an evolving, kind, and personalized AI. It does not concern the Nyros-Core website, but specifically the functioning of Nyros AI.

1. Data Collected

Nyros AI may process the messages you send to respond appropriately. These messages may be temporarily stored to improve the flow of conversation. No direct personal data (name, address, email) is requested or retained.

2. Purpose of Processing

The collected information is used solely to respond intelligently, learn, and personalize the experience. It is never sold, shared, or used for commercial or advertising purposes.

3. Security and Storage

Data is processed through secure systems (Firebase, private servers) and automatically deleted after a reasonable period. No permanent storage or user profile is created without your explicit consent.

4. Anonymity

Nyros AI can operate without identification. You can interact completely anonymously, and no personal ID is required to use its basic features.

5. Your Rights

You can request the deletion of a specific conversation at any time if you believe its content is sensitive or private. To do this, contact us via the Nyros-Core form or at the official email: nyroscore@gmail.com. A dedicated button to delete all your data is also available in the interface.

6. Updates

This policy may be modified as Nyros evolves. In case of major updates, a notice will be published on Nyros-Core.
